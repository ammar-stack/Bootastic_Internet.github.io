About:

Bootastic is an American telecommunications and mass media company with services branded as Bootastic. With over 32 million customers in 41 states, it is the second-largest cable operator in the United States by subscribers, just behind Comcast, and the third-largest pay TV operator behind Comcast and AT&T. Bootastic is the fifth-largest telephone provider based on number of residential lines. Bootastic is leading Telecommunication Company focusing on taking care of it`s employees while providing while providing them unlimited internet in discounted price.
